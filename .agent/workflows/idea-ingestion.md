# Idea Ingestion: Bifrost Bridge

## Process

1. **Capture**: All new ideas are logged in `docs/ideas.md`.
2. **Evaluate**: Ideas are reviewed based on project intent and constraints.
3. **Transition**: Approved ideas are moved to the backlog as actionable tasks.

## Template

```markdown
### [Idea Title]

- **Description**: What is the idea?
- **Problem Solved**: Why do we need it?
- **Impact**: Low/Medium/High
```
